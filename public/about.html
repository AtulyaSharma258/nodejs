Supplying Quality Pipes & Fittings Since 1984

Krishna Water Supply Company has been a trusted name in the piping and water supply industry for over four decades. Founded in 1984 in Sahibabad, we’ve grown from a family business into a leading supplier of high-quality pipes and fittings for water, industrial, and construction needs across North India.

Our commitment is simple: provide the best products, honest service, and expert support. Whether you need MS Casing Pipes, Column Pipes, Slotted Pipes, or Flange Fittings, we deliver only the most reliable, certified products on the market. Our customers include government agencies, contractors, builders, and industries who value long-term performance and trustworthy supply.

Over the years, we’ve supplied materials to major public and private sector projects—earning a reputation for on-time delivery, technical expertise, and after-sales support. Our experienced team is always ready to help you choose the right solution, big or small.

As a family-run business, our word is our bond. When you partner with Krishna Water Supply Company, you’re getting quality, reliability, and a relationship that lasts long after the sale.
